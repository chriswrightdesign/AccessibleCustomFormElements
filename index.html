Custom form elements

A collection of form elements created to work across browsers and devices, and maintain accessibility by still allowing keyboard control and focus.

Warning: This project is still in an experimental stage, and needs to be tested with VoiceOver, JAWS, and NVDA to confirm they're still actually properly accessible.
